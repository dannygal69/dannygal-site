Start-Up Founders Coaching

Emphasis on Building Healthy Relationships Between Co-Founders

👥

For Whom?

  • Partner founders in early startup stages (pre-seed and seed)
  • Investors and funds interested in strengthening founders and increasing the success chances of their investment
⏱️

When?

  • When tensions and disagreements emerge in management style
  • When the initial psychological contract between founders doesn't fit reality in the field
  • When division of roles and responsibilities is not clear enough
  • When communication requires work
📅

Duration

3 Months

The Process

1

Individual Conversations

With each of the founders

2

Joint Conversations

In couples therapy or family therapy format

3

Redefinition

Of the psychological contract and R&R (Roles and Responsibilities)

4

New Beginning

Building management and sharing the burden among additional partners beyond the founders

Build a Strong Founding Partnership

The right relationship between founders is the foundation of every successful company
