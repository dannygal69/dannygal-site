Diversity, Equity, Inclusion & Belonging

DEI&B

Diversity without inclusion creates loneliness. Inclusion without belonging creates pressure to assimilate.

👥

For Whom?

  • Organizations interested in strengthening diversity and belonging
⏱️

When?

  • When you want diversity but don't receive enough CVs from diverse candidates
  • When diverse employees don't feel belonging
  • When diverse employees are only on the production floor and don't advance to management positions
  • When the global organization pushes for implementing DEI&B strategy in your region
  • When internal conflicts and tensions arise as a result of diversity
  • When you want to leverage the advantages of diversity to achieve business results
📅

Duration

6–12 Months

The Process

1

Diagnosis of Current State

In DEI&B areas and define the stage where the organization is

2

Establish Steering Team

To lead the topic and guide it

3

Strategic Workshop for Management

To get acquainted with the topic and challenges

4

Establish Action Team

To identify diversity populations and build ways to reach them to the organization

5

Build Career Development

And codes for diverse employees

6

Create Internal Diversity Communities

Within the organization

7

Create Set of Metrics

And manager evaluation related to diversity achievements

8

Conduct Periodic Belonging Surveys

And continuous learning for required improvement and change

Based on insights from The Belonging Paradox
