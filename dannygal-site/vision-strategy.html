Strategic Impact

Vision, Strategy and Impact

Developing the Organization's Unique Contribution to the World

👥

For Whom?

  • Senior management teams
  • Extended management
  • Meetings with all stakeholders
⏱️

When?

  • When the meaning is not clear to employees and even to managers
  • When there are conflicts between stakeholders (board, customers, community, suppliers, the organization itself)
  • When external criticism begins to affect functioning and results
  • When managers have personal values that don't necessarily align with the organization's activities
  • When there's a problem with employee engagement due to lack of meaning
📅

Duration

6 Months

The Process

1

Analyze the Organizational Story

From its founding with a narrative approach

2

Employee Attitude Diagnosis

Or analysis of existing data

3

Meaning and Vision Workshop

For senior management

4

Workshop for Extended Management

To align and engage broader leadership

5

Establish Task Forces

Whose role is to align and change key processes in the organization according to the defined vision

Clarify Your Organization's Why

Reconnect your people to a shared sense of meaning and direction
